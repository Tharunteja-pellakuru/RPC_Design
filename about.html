The Heart Behind the Vision

Dr. Venky Williams Pellakuru

Royal Priest Church wasn't born out of a desire for expansion, but from a quiet moment of clarity. In 2008, Dr. Venky Williams Pellakuru envisioned a sanctuary that bridged the gap between timeless tradition and the complexities of modern life.

"I saw a world that was increasingly connected yet deeply lonely," Julian reflects. "Our goal was to create a digital and physical space that felt like a sun-drenched cathedral—a place where silence has a voice and every individual is treated with the dignity of royalty."

"We are not just building a congregation; we are restoring a sense of sacred identity in every soul that walks through our doors."

Our Mission
