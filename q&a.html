Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our church, our community, and how you can join us in worship and service.

What should I expect during a Sunday service?

add
Our services typically last about 75 minutes. They include contemporary worship music, a time of prayer, and a practical, Bible-based message designed to help you grow in your faith and navigate daily life. We aim for a reverent yet warm atmosphere where everyone feels welcome.

Is there a specific dress code for your church?

add
At Royal Priest Church, we believe God looks at the heart rather than outward appearances. You'll find people in everything from business casual to jeans and t-shirts. Please wear what makes you feel comfortable and respectful.

Do you have programs for children and youth?

add
Absolutely. We have a vibrant Kids Ministry for ages 0-11 and a Youth Collective for middle and high school students. Our programs are designed to be engaging, safe, and spiritually nurturing, allowing parents to enjoy the main service while their children learn in a tailored environment.

How can I get involved in a small group?

add
Community happens in circles, not just rows. You can join a 'Kingdom Group' by visiting our Connect Table in the lobby after service, or by browsing our digital directory on the website. We have groups for various life stages and interests that meet throughout the week.

How does the church support the local community?

add
We are committed to being "The Modern Sanctuary" for our city. Through our outreach programs, we partner with local food banks, provide tutoring services, and host monthly community events. A portion of every gift to the church is directly reinvested into local mission initiatives.

Still have questions?

We'd love to hear from you. Our team is here to help you find your place in our community.
